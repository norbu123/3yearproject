Currency Recognition and Fake Currency Detection      

This system focuses on identifying and categorizing the Ngultrum, the Bhutanese currency.The list of currencies contains the following denominations: Nu. 1, 5, 10, 20, 50, 100, 500, and 1000
Additionally, it has the ability to recognize fake Indian currency.

Aim

To build an application that can classify Bhutanese currency notes (except old). Along with that we also aim to identify counterfeit Indian notes.

About Project



Welcome to our website which utilizes the power of deep learning technology to classify Bhutanese Ngultrum and also detect counterfeit Indian banknotes. Our website is designed to provide a simple and efficient solution for anyone who wants to quickly and accurately determine the authenticity of their banknotes. With just a few clicks, you can easily upload an image of your banknote and our system will do the tricks.
